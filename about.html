About Us

Founded in 2005 in Lebanon, ConstraBuild has been at the forefront of the construction industry, where commitment meets excellence. As a seasoned General Contractor and Construction Manager, we specialize in turnkey solutions for high-tech buildings and offer meticulous constructibility reviews for discerning Owners and Architects. Our dedication to delivering excellence in every project has established us as pioneers in the industry.

Our journey has been defined by an unwavering dedication to two core principles:

  • Delivering unparalleled quality in construction services and products.
  • Meticulously managing projects to ensure they are completed on time and within budget constraints.

At ConstraBuild, our commitment extends beyond constructing buildings; we foster trust, ensure reliability, and cultivate enduring relationships with our clients.

With a track record of performance and a commitment to innovation, we stand ready to turn your vision into reality, one project at a time.

Our Leadership

Under the visionary leadership of our Founder and CEO, Nibal Jawhar, ConstraBuild has built a sound reputation for performance and commitment to excellence.
