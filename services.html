General Contracting

General contracting is the most effective mode of operation for projects with a well-defined scope. We work primarily as a General Contractor for all types of civil and electro-mechanical works, specifically tailored for each client:

  • Highrise Buildings
  • Offices and Commercial Towers
  • Villas & Private Residences
  • Military Compounds

Project & Construction Management

Project & Construction Management is an excellent vehicle for fast-track operations; it maximizes competition, is flexible in dealing with unforeseen changes, and allows Clients to save costs through the design and construction phases:

  • Overall Project Management
  • Value Engineering & Constructability Studies
  • Quality, Cost and Time Control
  • Site Management and Coordination of Subcontractors

Residential Renovation

We specialize in transforming distressed properties into beautiful and functional homes. Our residential renovation services cover:

  • Complete Home Restoration
  • Structural Repairs
  • Foundation Reinforcement
  • Interior and Exterior Rehabilitation
